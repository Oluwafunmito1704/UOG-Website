The lack of a digital literacy in communities deprives millions of people of opportunities.

Let's Fix That Together

Innovative approaches to a profound and intricate issue.

Assisting communities in bridging the technological gap.

Our belief is that every individual requiring digital knowledge and skills should have access to them, as digital skills lie at the core of achieving digital fairness. Acquiring these abilities and the knowledge to use them effectively is an essential and fundamental requirement in the modern world.
With a population of over 200 million in Nigeria, over half of them lack any understanding of the digital realm. This lack of digital literacy hampers their access to education, telehealth, and employment opportunities. To address this issue, Union of Genius, a nonprofit organization, is dedicated to providing equal opportunities for acquiring digital skills to those who have been financially restricted from accessing such learning opportunities.

Our Supporters

Donation Facilitation

With numerous generous contributions from individuals and corporations, we can annually enhance digital literacy in various communities by approximately 40% more than the standard rate. This empowers each individual with valuable skills that can enable them to excel in the technology sector.

Digital Opportunity Network

Our network consists of corporations, individuals, nonprofit organizations, schools, post-secondary institutions, state government, digital training companies, and like-minded individuals. Together, we collaborate to create a local impact in communities across the nation.

Changing the Future

For decades, the digital divide has persisted, acting as a hindrance to success for millions in our country. Digital skills serve as the essential key that opens up vast opportunities. Let us unite and collaborate to ensure that everyone becomes digitally literate.
